# Objective

Establish that showme connects curated knowledge, an explicit visual identity
and human review into one presentation workflow.

# Suggested content

Use one short headline, one sentence of explanation and a simple three-part
visual relationship:

1. Context — what the deck is allowed to say.
2. Design — how the deck should look and feel.
3. Review — where a person approves the result.

# Constraints

- Do not claim that showme eliminates human review.
- Do not introduce provider names or performance numbers without a cited
  concept in the bundle.
- Keep the slide readable from a distance and leave space for citations in the
  review view.
