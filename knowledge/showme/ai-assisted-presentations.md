# Problem

Creating a coherent presentation requires organizing a narrative, selecting
evidence and applying a visual system at the same time. Starting from a blank
canvas makes those decisions expensive and difficult to audit.

# Promise

Showme turns a goal and a curated knowledge bundle into a draft storyboard and
slides. Every generated claim remains connected to its source context so a
person can review and revise it.

# Constraints

- Generated content is a proposal until a person approves it.
- Unsupported factual claims must be marked for review.
- The visual system comes from `DESIGN.md`, not from arbitrary model output.
